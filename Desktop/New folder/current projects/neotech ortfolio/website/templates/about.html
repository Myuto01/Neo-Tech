NEO-TECH is a technology company based in Lusaka, Zambia. Founded in 2022 by Mapalo Chiti and Mutale C. Mwango as an outlet for technological innovations that will improve the lives of Africans. Neotech will produce software for clients as well as innovate (as is with our first project). The market for software has stood the test of time and is looking to continue that trend. The biggest companies in todays day and age are based around application programs and software development. We are also engaged in Blockchain development. Our work with Blockchain will be soon on show as our next project is in this space. With the rise of artificial intelligence it has brought with it a whole new market. AI products are in demand because of the value they provide in form of efficiency. We are looking to be at the forefront of innovation in this field. Overtime we will be involved in robotics. This is an area that is not capitalized on in our continent despite it being viable. We will produce technology that can be used in farming, mining, multimedia and journalism. They can be used as military equipment and have uses in almost all other major industries. Overall robotic use in society is getting much more popular as this is an age where man and machine are beginning to integrate. Microchips are also a big player in our day to day technology. They are the brains of everyday computers in everything from cars to laptops and so much more.

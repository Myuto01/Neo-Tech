Meeting Procedure

Only book a meeting when you have a project you want us to work on.
For questions, go to the contact page.

Step1: Schedule a meeting with us by clicking the "Book Meeting" icon above.

Step2: Enter the date and time you would like the meeting to take place.

Step3: Once you have booked the meeting, NEO-TECH will contact you immediately to confirm the date and time.

Step4: On the day of the meeting, you are expected to be ready by the time scheduled.

Step5: During the meeting your project will be discussed in detail as well as the time it will take to complete the project.

Neo-tickets
--------------------------

While using neo-tickets, situations may arise where the user may resort to cancelling the reservation and requesting for a refund. Depending on the time of the request we will deduct a penalty fee. If a request is made 48 hours before departure 5% will be deducted. If a request is made 6 hours before departure 25% will be deducted. If a request is made 2 hours before departure 50% will be deducted. In a case where a request is made 1 hour before departure the request will be admissible.

Cancellation of tickets is allowed only up to 1 hour before the scheduled departure of the bus service from the bus station. After that cancellation will not be allowed. Further, cancellation can be done only before 9 PM for the current date journey inclusive of the next day's journey before 7 AM.

All refunds related to neo-tickets will be refunded to the concerned Credit card / Bank Account only within 3 to 7 bank working days. The time taken for transfer of amount to user account depends on the concerned bank's policy. Neo-tickets will not be responsible for the bankside delay. In case the refund is not received please contact us through the support button located at the bottom right of the app or call us on 0973718615.
Alternatively contact us on neosupport@gmail.com
